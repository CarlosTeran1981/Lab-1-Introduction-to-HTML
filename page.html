My thoughts on Game of Thrones

I was a fan of this show since the first episode. It contained lots of action and drama. FIght scenes were amazing. I still remember in season five when "The Mountain" killed Oberyn Martell by crushing his skull. However, I was not a fan of the last season.

The Ending

I felt that the last season was rushed into six episodes. Although I must say that the third episode "The Long Night" was the best of that season. I feel that this season should've been staged throughout 8 episodes at minimum.
